Adopt a Pet

Across New York City, there are thousands and thousands of homeless animals. Many are a result of pets not being spayed or neutered and some were abandoned. Regardless, there is something everyone can do to help, and that is adoption. If you're on the fence, there are numerous health benefits to owning a pet.

Benefits of Adopting

  • Decreased blood pressure
  • Decreased feelings of loneliness
  • Decreased levels of stress
  • Lowers cholesterol
  • Improves mood
